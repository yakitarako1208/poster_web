Walk n' Snap

ISDW Group7

In our daily lives, we engage in countless conversations, but as time passes, it is easy to forget them.

A wearable device helps overcome this limitation by recording memorable or important moments in conversations, allowing them to be remembered.

Withing the recorded content, users can easily find and revisit key moments, ensuring that the essence of long conversations is not missed.
